About us, The Service Stop

Our founders always felt, there is a need for a trustable repairing service. A place where people can leave their valuable and costly products without any hasitation.


When almost every one is looting the customers by the name of repairing, we decided to bring a change. So, in the year of 2016, we started a service "The Service Stop".


We not only handle customer's products with care and responsibility but also provide service with a reasonable price.


Our team consists of highly talented and well experienced professionals. 100% of the time we try to give our best in order to fix a product.


Currently, we are providing repairing service for all apple devices and all other popular brands like dell, HP, Lenevo etc.
